About

Rabbit Reverie is a small handmade brand by Michelle, blending cottagecore textures, lace, and cute character design into sweet, wearable treasures.

Each piece is crafted with care in California — from tapestry ribbon headdresses and ribbon-rose combs to felt magnets and vinyl stickers. Made to spark comfort, softness, and a little bit of daydream.
